SpotifyMe

SpotifyMe is a web application with a Python Flask backend. This web application is designed to connect to Spotify API using "User Authorization Flow Code". Capturing your latest playlist and some other basic information enforces us to build analysis and recommendations to enhance your spotify experience. You will be able to see also some more detailed analysis of the your taste and mood of music

Please give us the possibility to access your data.
